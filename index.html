💡 Recycling Tips

Donate working electronics to NGOs.
Take broken devices to certified recyclers.
Sell old gadgets to authorized resellers.
Never throw e-waste in regular bins.

🌍 Why E-Waste Tracker?

Every year, tons of electronic waste are dumped improperly, harming the environment. Our tracker helps spread awareness and keeps a record of reported e-waste to encourage safe recycling.
